Matar Haller

I currently am a data scientist at Winton Capital in San Francisco. Prior to working at Winton, I was a data science fellow at Insight Data Science where I engineered an algorithm for automatic speaker segmentation from audio streams.

I did my PhD in Dr. Robert Knight's laboratory at the Helen Wills Neuroscience Institute at the University of California at Berkeley, where I studied the link between perception and action by recording activity from electrodes placed directly on the surface of the human brain.

Before my PhD, I was a preschool teacher and a tank instructor in the military (not at the same time!).
