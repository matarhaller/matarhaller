Matar Haller

I am a data scientist at Winton Capital in San Francisco. Prior to working at Winton, I was a fellow at Insight Data Science where I engineered an algorithm for automatic speech segmentation from audio streams.

I did my PhD in Dr. Robert Knight's laboratory at the Helen Wills Neuroscience Institute at the University of California at Berkeley, where I studied the link between perception and action by recording activity from electrodes placed directly on the surface of human brains.

Before my PhD, I was a preschool teacher and a tank instructor (not at the same time!). I am passionate about expanding opportunties for women in STEM fields, and I am a mother to an amazing toddler who surprises me every day.
