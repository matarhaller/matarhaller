Matar Haller

I am a senior data scientist at SparkBeyond, where we are building an automated research engine to solve the world's most impactful problems. Prior to SparkBeyond, I was a data scientist at Winton Capital, where I explored signals in diverse datasets. I was also a fellow at Insight Data Science where I engineered an algorithm for automatic speech segmentation from audio streams.

I completed my PhD in Dr. Robert Knight's laboratory at the Helen Wills Neuroscience Institute at the University of California at Berkeley, where I studied the link between perception and action by recording activity from electrodes surgically placed on the surface of human brains.

Before my PhD, I was a preschool teacher and a tank instructor (not at the same time!). I am passionate about expanding opportunities for women in STEM fields and am a mother to two wonderfuls children who surprise and inspire me every day.
